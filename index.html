Our Mission

Rooted in Utah. Devoted to giving back.

Klean was founded in the shadow of the Wasatch, where mountains meet desert and a quiet, singular lake holds an entire ecosystem together. We're a Utah-based company, through and through — and giving back to the community and land that built us isn't a tagline, it's the reason we started.

Right now, that means standing behind the Great Salt Lake, which has lost over half its surface area and, with it, the habitat millions of migratory birds and our region's air quality depend on. We even harvest real mineral salt from the lake itself as an active ingredient in every bottle — so the connection isn't just financial, it's formulated in. A portion of every sale goes directly to charities working on its restoration, so your everyday hair routine becomes a small act of preservation.
